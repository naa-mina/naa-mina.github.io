Project 1: Rwanda Map (PDF)

This map visualizes population coverage in relation to health facilities and illustrates Zipline’s delivery coverage across all three distribution centers in Rwanda.

Project 2: Updated Lesotho Map (PDF)

This analysis explores optimal locations for placing a distribution center in Lesotho. It considers the elevation constraint for drone deliveries (maximum altitude: 2850m) and estimates population coverage from various locations. The chosen site, located near Rapokolana, offers the most effective reach within these parameters.
